MY PROJECTS AND PSR

I believe in PSR ( Personal Social Responsibility ) - a term which I coined that refers to the duties of a capable individual towards the betterment of the society. By 'capable' , I suggest physical , intellectual and financial capability. I firmly believe that like big corporates , individuals must also give back to the society whenever, wherever and in whatever magnitudes . 

THALPE RECYCLING PICKUP SERVICE
With the help of the community-minded expatriates and locals, I was able to launch the first independent recycling pickup service which operates from Bentota to Hiriketiya. We serve both businesses and homes. The primary aim of the service is to direct the community towards correct waste segregation and responsible disposal. Two years and counting !

CLASSROOM AND LIBRARY DEVELOPMENT
"A proper class , A proper lesson" Many schools in Sri Lanka lack proper classroom facilities which hinders a holistic education. My aim is to improve the English learning and library facilities of rural schools in the South. So far I have succeeded in completing classrooms and libraries at U/Ketawala Kanishta Vidyalaya , Mandalapura Vidyalaya and Agalawatta Kanishta Vidyalaya with the support of the community. 

SUPPORTING SCHOOL CRICKET
Cricket is a hobby as well as a passion. I firmly believe that this game could change the lives of the local children. Therefore , I have started to support the senior and junior cricket teams of B/Rathanajothi Maha Vidyalaya Galle by providing them with funds from International donors to purchase cricketing gear and rope matting. The team has already shown a positive change !

SOCIAL ENTERPRISE
I have identified 'Social Enterprise' as the best way to connect Tourism and Sustainability in the Sri Lankan context. Therefore , I have started the sustainable tourism platform Dee Lanka Wanderers which unveils the authentic Sri Lankan lifestyle and culture to worldwide visitors. The company's core values are recycling, gender equity , accessibility and circular economy.

PLASTIC POLLUTION AWARENESS RAISING
Using my bi-lingual capabilities and wide network of connections in the private and government sectors , I conduct and co-ordinate awareness lectures , clean-ups and immersive solid waste management sessions. In this venture , I have been able to create a bridge between different experts , their knowledge and the local community.
